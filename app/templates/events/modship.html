Man has always been a servant to his environment….amazed by how things around him work and persevere to make a better future…! Explore your inner psyche and plung into the depths of Innovation, Knowledge, Creativity and Fun.

Team Formation:

  • A team can have maximum of 2 students.
  • No student can be part of more than one team.
  • It is not necessary that the students forming a team should be from the same college.
  • There is no restriction on the number of teams from the same college

Event format:

The event will be sub-categorized into three different rounds:

Round 1:

  • A Quiz based on Aptitude, Reasoning and General Knowledge

Round 2:

  • Creative Drawing Session…..!

Round 3:

  • Ship Model Making on the spot….the Real test…!

Rules and Regulations:

Registration can be done by mailing the name and enroll of the team participants on civilengineersnitsri2013@gmail.com or by contacting the organizers (details given below)

  • Each Team should comprise of 2 to 3 participants
  • Candidates found using Mobile phones or any other electronic gadgets will be eliminated on the spot.
  • MCQs cum Objective questions based on General Science, Aptitude, Current Affairs, etc. will be asked in Quiz
  • Top rankers will be selected in first stage & second stage on the basis of points obtained in quiz & drawing respectively.
  • Chart paper will be provided in the 2nd stage & participants have to bring pencils, eraser, drafter or scale or etc. (required for drawing) own self.
  • All the required material will be provided in 3rd stage by us.
  • Every Stage will be judged by Senior Professors/ Faculty members and points will be allocated likewise.
  • Organizers decision will be final and binding in any dispute.
  • Organizers reserve the right to change any of the above rules or rules displayed on website they deem to be fit.
