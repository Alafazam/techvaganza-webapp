COOlING TOWER DESIGN

EVENT FORMAT:

DESCRIPTION AND SPECIFICATIONS:

Participants are expected to design a Natural Draft/Draft Cooling Tower model having the following specifications:

  • Cooling Tower Max.Dimensions = 30cms x 30cms x 30cms.
  • Material of Construction â€“ No Restriction.
  • Maximum Dry Weight of Tower = 4kg.
  • Total Water Pouring Time = 30 sec.
  • Quantity of Water to be Cooled = 750 ml
  • Minimum Collection of Water at the end of run = 200 ml.
  • Residence Time in Tower = 150 Seconds.
  • Maximum Permitted Loss = 15gm.
  • There must be an outlet for drainage/collection, along with a valve if possible. This is a compulsory condition.
  • No direct addition of cooling chemicals.

Minimum water loss will be given importance.

Please Note:-If any of the above instructions are violated the participant will be disqualified and will not be allowed to participate in the event.

Round1:

  • Elimination round:-In this round there will be quiz which will be related to chemical engineering and aptitude.

Round2:

  • Design round:-Top 8 teams of elimination round will qualify for this round, And they have to come up with their working model.

Rule:

  • Each participating team should comprise of maximum 3 or less members.
  • The decision of the judges and the event head will be treated as final and binding on all.
  • Organizer will reserve all the right to change rules.

Judgement Criteria:

  • Minimum temperature after 5 minutes of the working of the model.
  • Maximum water collection capacity.
  • The Material of Construction for the design.
  • Economic Considerations of the design.
  • Tie Breaker: In case of two models showing exactly the same drop in water temperature, preference will be given to the model which shows minimum weight loss and maximum water collection capacity.
