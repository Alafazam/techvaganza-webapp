Event details:

Problem statement:

First round is simple qualification round based on aptitude and simple programming. 10 Teams will be selected from 1st round and in the 2nd round will be related to flaw detection, and points gained in this round will contribute to the final round, and the final round is based on logical programming and swapping between team members after a given interval of time (CODE-CHAINING).

The vision of this event is to check your observation skills, logic, time management and Team Coordination.

Round1(the eliminator):

This is the qualifier round where the questions will be based on general programming and technical aptitude. The paper type will be objective and participants are required to choose the correct alternative among the four choices, whereupon ten teams will move on to the next round.

Round2(don't forget the bugs):

This is the knockout round for the finale where teams will be provided with 6 to 7 code snippets and the motive will be to form a simple numeric code by determining the number of flaws/errors in each snippet. Evaluation will be based upon the accuracy of your code. Thence, 5 teams will advance To the final event.

Round3(Code switching):

This is the ultimate battle level that is goanna check up your mutual understanding and team coordination. Three distinct problems will be provided to the different members of the team and after a specific interval, all three will be shuffled with some other member of the same team. You have to pick up from where your friend left and complete the code until next shuffle.

Note:

You are not allowed to use comments or communicate with your team-mate.

  • This event shall be preceded by a qualifying round as mentioned above.
  • 10 teams shall be selected for the second round depending on their performance in stage 1 and then 5 teams for the final stage.
