About Us

Techvaganza is the annual college festival of NIT Srinagar. Its an exceptional confluence of exquisite cultural displays with technological innovations reverberating in an aura filled with excitement, amusement and splendor. After the overwhelming success of Techvaganza 2014, NIT Srinagar is back with Techvaganza 2015 which promises to transcend all the thresholds of science and entertainment.Techvaganza 2015 guarantees inimitable world, teeming with boundless merriment, talent and skill as you plunge into the ocean of entertainment at this three day youth extravaganza.

What's in It

A roller-coaster of emotions in the CodeMania to an ideal SmackDown of Robots spread throughout the college, you get it all here at Techvaganza`15. In a nutshell, the three day extravaganza at Techvaganza will be a showcase of spectacular talent and an exhilarating experience for the populace. We have something for everyone - be it a Art Lover, a music fanatic, a Geek, a quiz wizard or simply a fun loving mortal. Fuelling the fire of passion, inquisitiveness and the desire for perfection with a topping of unique and challenging events ingeniously crafted by some of the smartest young minds of India. Feel the fire consume your soul as you compete against the best teams of the country!

Its not just a festival, its a celebration.
